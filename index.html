This website displays the locations Big Wands in End of Everything Great Chests around the map. Such wands are occasionally useful for obscure builds that require more than 26 slots.

The Great Chest wands can be obtained by getting a great chest to spawn at exactly the given coordinates. You can align an EoE cast at the pixel found by locking yourself in the approximate location, then checking your save00/player.xml.

Adjust how big is considered "Big" by changing the capacity option. Wands of greater or equal size will be searched for.

The farland button sets the search to locations where coordinate rounding allows for a larger than 1-pixel region to align the chest.
